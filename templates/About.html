About Todo App

The Todo App is a simple and intuitive task management application built using Flask and SQLAlchemy.

With the Todo App, you can organize your tasks effectively and never miss an important deadline again. Add new tasks, update their titles or descriptions, mark them as completed, and delete them when you're done.

Features:

  • Easy-to-use interface for adding and managing tasks
  • Automatically timestamps tasks with their creation date
  • Responsive design for use on desktop and mobile devices
  • Securely stores your tasks in a SQLite database

Whether you're managing personal to-dos or team projects, the Todo App is here to simplify your task management process.

Thank you for using our Todo App. We hope it helps you stay organized and boost your productivity.
